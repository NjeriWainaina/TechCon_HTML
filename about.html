History

TechCon was founded in 2010 to bring together technology enthusiasts, industry experts, and innovators from around the world. Over the years, it has grown into a global platform for sharing ideas, launching products, and building networks that shape the future of technology.

Mission

Our mission is to inspire and empower the tech community by creating opportunities for learning, collaboration, and innovation. We believe in making technology accessible, inclusive, and a force for good.

Past Speakers

Grace Hopper

Computer science pioneer and Navy Rear Admiral who helped develop early programming languages. Her insights into the history of computing set the tone for our first TechCon.

Elon Musk

CEO of Tesla and SpaceX, Elon discussed the future of AI and space travel at TechCon 2018, inspiring a generation of innovators.

Ginni Rometty

Former IBM CEO who shared her journey in transforming a century-old company into a leader in cloud and AI technologies.
